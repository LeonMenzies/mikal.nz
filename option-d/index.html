Who's Mikal?

The Man Behind
the Method

Born in Denmark, Mikal went from a super shy, withdrawn, and bullied child to a confident public speaker and coach — following a life-changing experience at age 27. He considers himself an "earth citizen, not belonging to any country, culture, religion or political orientation." He has travelled to over 50 countries.

For the past 30 years he has followed his calling as a life coach — helping people get more life out of life, more business out of business. He has written four books (two published) and appeared on national TV, radio, and in most national newspapers in New Zealand.

"If anyone ever accuses me of being normal, I'm going to get very offended."

He is a husband, father of three, café goer, and enthusiastic kitchen experimenter — to the delight or dismay of the family, depending on the experiment.
