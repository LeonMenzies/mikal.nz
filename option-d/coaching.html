Group Programme

The GPS Programme

An 8-week intensive group coaching experience. GPS — Growth, Performance, Success — is designed for people who are ready to do the real work alongside others on the same journey.

Eight weeks that can change everything

The GPS Programme is Mikal's flagship group offering. Delivered online, it brings together a carefully selected cohort of individuals committed to genuine personal growth. The group format adds a dimension that one-on-one work cannot — the power of shared experience, mutual accountability and collective insight.

Each week focuses on a core mental fitness pillar. Between sessions, you apply what you have learned in your real life, return with results and refine your approach in a supportive, no-fluff environment.

Private Retreats

An immersive solo retreat experience

For individuals who want an intensive, fully focused experience, Mikal offers exclusive private retreats in Northland, New Zealand. These are not relaxation retreats. They are designed to accelerate transformation through deep, sustained coaching work in a distraction-free environment.

You work directly and intensively with Mikal over multiple days, addressing the core patterns, beliefs and behaviours that are holding you back. The Northland setting provides the space, silence and natural power needed for genuine inner work.

What is included

  • --Pre-retreat assessment and goal setting
  • --Intensive daily coaching sessions with Mikal
  • --Accommodation and meals in the retreat setting
  • --Post-retreat integration and follow-up support
  • --A personalised framework for the months ahead
