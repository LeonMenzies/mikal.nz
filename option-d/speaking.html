Speaking

A speaker who changes the room

Mikal Nielsen brings more than 30 years of insight, lived experience and the rare ability to be both direct and deeply human. His sessions leave audiences not just informed, but genuinely shifted.

A Different Approach

Not your typical keynote speaker

Mikal describes himself as an un-motivational speaker. Not because he lacks energy — but because he refuses to leave an audience feeling temporarily pumped and permanently unchanged. His sessions are built on substance: real frameworks, real psychology and real conversations that continue long after the room empties.

He has spoken internationally to audiences ranging from intimate leadership gatherings to large conference stages. His presentations are customised to your audience and objectives — never recycled, never generic.

What every Mikal Nielsen session shares is intellectual honesty, genuine connection and a clear path forward that your people can walk.

The Core Message

The arrow always points back to mind

Every challenge an organisation faces — performance, culture, communication, leadership, stress, conflict — traces back to the mental fitness of the people in the room. Mikal's speaking brings that truth to life in a way that is not academic or abstract, but immediate and actionable.

His audiences leave with a clear understanding of their own mental patterns, practical tools to shift them and a belief — often new — that change is genuinely within reach. That is the arrow. It always points to the mind.

Topics

Keynote topics and themes

All presentations are customised for your audience. These are the core themes Mikal speaks to, drawn from decades of research and real-world coaching.

Performance

Mental Fitness: The Competitive Edge

Why the organisations and individuals who invest in mental fitness consistently outperform those who don't. A compelling case for the inner game of professional excellence.

Leadership

Leading from the Inside Out

Great leadership begins with self-leadership. Mikal explores the mental habits, beliefs and blind spots that separate good leaders from exceptional ones.

Productivity

Time Management: A Complete Waste of Time

Based on his bestselling book — a provocative, research-backed dismantling of the productivity myth and a new framework for what actually produces results.

Resilience

Performing Under Pressure

What separates those who thrive in high-pressure environments from those who buckle. Practical mental fitness tools for individuals and teams facing real demands.

Culture

Building a Mentally Fit Team

Culture is upstream of everything. This session gives leaders and teams the language, tools and shared commitment needed to build a genuinely high-performing culture.

Wellbeing

Stress, Burnout and the Path Back

An honest, practical session on the real causes of workplace stress and burnout — and the mental fitness strategies that create sustainable wellbeing and performance.
