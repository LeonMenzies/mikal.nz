Self-Assessment

Discover your inner saboteurs

We all have inner mental patterns — automatic responses, beliefs and behaviours — that hold us back without our awareness. These are called saboteurs, and they operate beneath the surface of even the most capable, intelligent people.

The Saboteur Assessment is a short, research-backed test that reveals your dominant saboteurs — the specific mental patterns most likely to be limiting your performance and happiness. Understanding them is the first step to overcoming them.

The assessment takes approximately 10 minutes and delivers immediate, personalised results.
