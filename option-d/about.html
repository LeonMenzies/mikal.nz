About

Meet Mikal Nielsen

Mental fitness coach, author, speaker and lifelong student of human potential — based in Ruakaka, Northland, New Zealand.

His Story

Thirty years in the pursuit of what makes people perform

Mikal Nielsen has spent more than three decades studying the inner world of human performance. His journey has taken him across more than 50 countries, working with individuals from every walk of life — from corporate executives and professional athletes to parents navigating midlife, and leaders on the edge of burnout.

Born with an insatiable curiosity about why people do what they do, Mikal became a student of psychology, behaviour, communication and the emerging field of mental fitness long before it entered the mainstream conversation. He built his practice not from theory, but from thousands of hours of real coaching conversations, retreats and speaking engagements.

Today he lives and works from Ruakaka in Northland, New Zealand — a place whose natural grandeur reflects the space and clarity he helps his clients find in themselves.

What Drives Him

A belief that every person contains more than they have yet discovered

Mikal is not interested in quick fixes or motivational bumper stickers. He is interested in the deep work — the kind that requires honesty, courage and sustained effort. His coaching is characterised by directness, warmth and an unwillingness to let his clients settle for less than they are capable of.

He believes mental fitness is the foundation of everything: better relationships, better leadership, better performance and a better life. It is not an add-on. It is the core.

"I have never met a person who could not do better. I have met many who did not believe they could. That is the only gap I work to close."Mikal Nielsen
Recognition

Professional credentials and community

His work has been recognised across New Zealand and internationally, with clients and audiences consistently rating his sessions among the most impactful they have experienced.

He is a proud New Zealander who believes Kiwi resilience and no-nonsense values translate powerfully into genuine personal growth.
