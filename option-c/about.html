About Mikal

A Life Spent in
Service of Growth

New Zealand-born. Globally shaped. Deeply rooted. Mikal Nielsen has spent over 30 years helping people discover what they are truly capable of.

His Story

Born in New Zealand. Shaped by the World.

Mikal Nielsen grew up in New Zealand, a country whose landscape teaches patience, perspective and respect for what is larger than yourself. These are not just metaphors for him. They have been the ground beneath his life's work.

Over three decades of coaching, Mikal has worked alongside thousands of people across every walk of life - entrepreneurs building something new, executives navigating pressure, parents finding their footing, individuals searching for meaning in the second half of life. What they all share is a desire to live more fully and more intentionally.

His curiosity has taken him to more than 50 countries, where he has studied different cultures, wisdom traditions and ways of understanding the human condition. That breadth has made him a rare kind of guide - one who brings genuine perspective without imposing a single map on another person's journey.

"I have never met two people with the same map. My job is not to give you mine. It is to help you read your own." Mikal Nielsen

The Person Behind the Work

Grounded in Family, Faith and the Long View

Mikal lives in Ruakaka in the Northland region of New Zealand, a place of coastline, sky and space. His home life is deeply important to him. He is a family man in the truest sense - not as a performance, but as a practice. He believes that the quality of your closest relationships is the clearest mirror of your mental fitness.

He brings a spiritual depth to his work that is not affiliated with any particular tradition. It is more fundamental than that - a respect for the inner life, for silence, for what happens when a person stops running and finally sits with themselves honestly.

That depth is what distinguishes Mikal's coaching from performance-focused approaches. He is not interested in helping you become more productive at the expense of who you are. He is interested in helping you become more fully yourself.

His Why

What Drives Mikal

Mikal has seen too many brilliant people living far below their potential - not because they lack talent or drive, but because no one ever showed them how to work on the most important thing: themselves.

He is driven by a conviction that the world becomes better one mind at a time. When a person becomes mentally fitter - calmer, clearer, more intentional - that ripples outward into their family, their team, their community.

This is not a career to Mikal. It is a calling. And he pursues it with the same rigour and honesty he asks of every person he works with.

Credentials

Experience, Recognition and Reach

30+ Years Coaching

Over three decades of structured, professional coaching experience across individuals, leadership teams, businesses and retreat contexts. Thousands of clients. Every kind of challenge imaginable.

Listed on Speakers New Zealand

What Mikal Stands For

Values That Shape Every Engagement

Honesty Over Comfort

Mikal will not tell you what you want to hear. He will tell you what he genuinely sees. This is the foundation of real coaching - not flattery, but honest, respectful truth.

Depth Over Speed

Lasting change takes time. Mikal is not interested in 5-step blueprints that promise transformation in a weekend. He is interested in work that sticks - because it is built on real understanding.

The Whole Person

Performance, relationships, purpose, health, spirituality - Mikal does not divide these into separate categories. He works with you as a whole human being, not a set of professional problems to solve.

Nature as Teacher

Living in Northland, surrounded by coastline and farmland, has reinforced what Mikal has always believed - that nature has much to teach us about cycles, patience and what it means to be alive.

In Action

On Stage and In the Room

Whether speaking to 300 business leaders or sitting with one person in a retreat setting, Mikal brings the same quality of presence: attentive, curious and completely unhurried.

His speaking style has been described as "tour de force" and "spellbinding" by event organisers. His coaching style has been described as "a rare gift to see what you cannot see in yourself" by those who have experienced it.

Both are true. And both emerge from the same source - a genuine love for people and an abiding belief that every person has far more in them than they currently see.
