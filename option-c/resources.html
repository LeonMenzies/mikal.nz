Mikal has distilled decades of coaching experience into a set of free, practical guides. No fluff. No gimmicks. Just honest, grounded tools you can use today.

Where Would You Like to Start?

Each guide is a standalone resource built around a specific area of mental fitness. Click the download button on any guide and you will be taken to a short contact form to receive your copy directly from Mikal.

Guide 1

Stress Buster Guide

Stress is not the enemy. Unmanaged stress is. This guide helps you understand where your stress actually comes from, and gives you a clear, practical framework for addressing it at the source rather than managing symptoms indefinitely.

Ideal for: anyone feeling overwhelmed, burnt out or persistently anxious.

Download Free Guide

Guide 2

Mental Fitness Guide

What is mental fitness, really? And how do you build it? This foundational guide introduces Mikal's core framework for mental fitness and gives you a clear starting point for your own practice - whether you are completely new to this or have been working on it for years.

Ideal for: anyone new to mental fitness or wanting a solid foundation.

Download Free Guide

Guide 3

Parenting Power Guide

Raising mentally strong children is one of the most important things a parent can do - and one of the least talked about. This guide shares Mikal's approach to parenting with depth, honesty and intention, giving you practical tools to support your children's mental fitness from the ground up.

Ideal for: parents of children of all ages seeking grounded, practical guidance.

Download Free Guide

Guide 4

Time Mastery Framework

A companion to Mikal's book, this guide introduces the Time Mastery Framework - the alternative to traditional time management that actually works. It reframes your relationship with time from scarcity and control to intention and meaning.

Ideal for: busy people who are productive but unfulfilled, or struggling with overwhelm.

Download Free Guide

External Resource

Discover Your Mental Saboteurs

One of the most powerful tools in the Positive Intelligence framework is the Saboteur assessment. Your saboteurs are the mental patterns - internal voices and habits - that undermine your performance, your relationships and your wellbeing, often without you even realising they are operating.

The assessment takes about 10 minutes and gives you a clear picture of which saboteurs are most active in your life. Mikal uses this as a starting point in many of his coaching relationships. It is free and highly revealing.
