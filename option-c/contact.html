Applying for Coaching

How the Coaching Application Works

Mikal works with a small number of coaching clients at any one time. This is deliberate - it allows him to give each person his full attention and care.

To explore personal coaching, begin by sending a message using the form alongside this section. Select "Personal Coaching" as your topic and tell Mikal a little about where you are and what you are hoping for.

From there, Mikal will invite you to a free 20-minute conversation to explore fit. No commitment required. No pressure.
