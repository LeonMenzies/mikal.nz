Keynote Speaker & Workshop Facilitator

Not Motivation.
Something That Lasts.

Mikal Nielsen calls himself an "un-motivational speaker" - and means it. His talks do not pump audiences up for a day and then leave them empty. They shift something at a deeper level.

What Makes Mikal Different

The Un-Motivational Speaker

Most motivational speakers work on the surface. They use energy, stories and humour to create a feeling in the room - and that feeling is real. But it rarely survives the commute home.

Mikal takes a fundamentally different approach. He addresses the thinking beneath the behaviour. He challenges the assumptions that most speakers would never touch, because they are uncomfortable. He earns the right to go there through his depth of preparation, his 30+ years of experience and his genuine respect for every person in the audience.

The result is a talk that people are still referring to weeks and months later. Not because it was memorable in a superficial sense - but because it changed something real.

A Signature Story

The Arrow-Breaking Story

One of the most talked-about moments in Mikal's speaking career is his arrow-breaking demonstration. It is a physical act - placing the sharpened tip of an arrow against his throat and breaking it with forward movement alone - but it is not about the arrow.

It is about belief. About the stories we tell ourselves about what is possible and what is not. About the invisible barriers we treat as solid walls.

When an audience member steps forward to do it themselves - and breaks through - the room changes. Theory becomes experience. Understanding becomes embodied knowledge.

"The arrow-breaking story completely changed the way I understand belief and what holds people back. I still think about it regularly."

Fiona Jones

Keynote Topics

What Mikal Speaks About

Each keynote can be tailored to your audience, your event theme and your desired outcomes. All topics are available as keynotes or half-day workshops.

Signature Keynote

Time Management: A Complete Waste of Time

Based on his bestselling book, this keynote dismantles the 7 myths of time management and introduces the Life Mastery Framework. Audiences leave with a fundamentally different understanding of how they use time - and why productivity without purpose is just busyness.

Leadership

Mental Fitness for Business Leaders

High performance without mental fitness is unsustainable. This keynote explores the mental habits, beliefs and practices that separate consistently effective leaders from those who burn out or plateau. Practical, honest and deeply relevant to the pressures of modern leadership.

Mindset

The Revolution in Thinking

What if the biggest obstacle to everything you want to achieve is not circumstances or competition - but the way you think? This keynote explores the revolution that happens when people begin to challenge their own assumptions. Challenging, liberating and deeply practical.

Wellbeing

Purpose, Performance and Peace

The three things most people want most - purpose, high performance and inner peace - are not in competition with each other. This keynote shows how they are interdependent and what it takes to build a life and career that honours all three simultaneously.

Productivity

The 7 Myths of Time Management

A tighter, sharper version of the time management keynote - ideal for conference breakout sessions or team workshops. Mikal walks through seven widely held beliefs about time and productivity and shows, step by step, why they are keeping people stuck.

Stress & Resilience

Stress: The Silent Business Killer

Stress is the single most pervasive and underaddressed challenge in modern workplaces. This keynote names it honestly, explores where it actually comes from and gives audiences practical tools to address it at the source - not just manage the symptoms.
