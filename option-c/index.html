The Story Behind the Work

From a bullied child in Denmark
to coaching leaders worldwide

Mikal Nielsen went from super shy, withdrawn and bullied child to confident public speaker, presenter and coach — following a transformative experience at age 27 that set him on a 35+ year journey of discovery.

"If you want a normal life, I'm definitely the wrong person for you. But if you are after extra-ordinary, I could be the one to help you discover your own key — and more importantly, how to use it to unlock what you are truly here in life to be and do."

An earth citizen who has visited over 50 countries, Mikal married a New Zealander and now calls Ruakaka, Northland home. His coaching is focused on depth rather than width — and it has changed the lives of clients across New Zealand and internationally.
