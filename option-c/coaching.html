One on One

Personal Coaching

Personal coaching with Mikal is for people who are serious about change. Not curious about it. Not considering it. Ready for it. These are people who have often already tried other approaches and sensed that something deeper was needed.

Mikal works with individuals across a wide range of contexts: business owners carrying the weight of their companies, professionals who have achieved much but feel hollow inside, people navigating major life transitions, and those who simply know they are not living at the level they are capable of.

Sessions are conducted via Zoom and, where possible, face-to-face in Auckland and Northland. The work is structured but responsive - Mikal will follow the thread that matters most in any given session, not a rigid curriculum.

What to Expect

  • Deep listening and honest reflection from Mikal
  • Work across all dimensions of life - not just one area
  • Practical tools and frameworks you can use immediately
  • Accountability and structure without rigidity
  • A relationship built on genuine respect and care

Small Group Programme

The GPS Group Programme

Growth. Purpose. Strategy. The GPS Programme is an intensive 8-week group coaching experience for a maximum of 12 people. It is designed for people who want the depth of coaching but also the richness of learning alongside others navigating similar terrain.

Programme Overview

The GPS Group Programme runs over 8 weeks with live sessions held every Wednesday from 8:00am to 9:30am New Zealand time. Sessions are held via Zoom, making the programme accessible to participants wherever they are in the world.

Each session is structured around a core theme - from mental fitness foundations to purpose clarity, from stress mastery to strategic thinking - but Mikal creates space for the real conversations that arise in the room. The group dynamic is one of the most valuable elements of the programme.

Between sessions, participants are given practical exercises and frameworks to work with in their own lives. The goal is not insight alone. It is insight converted into action.

What GPS Covers

  • The foundations of mental fitness and why they matter
  • Identifying and understanding your mental saboteurs
  • Clarifying your purpose and what actually drives you
  • Building resilience without bypassing genuine difficulty
  • Time and energy mastery - working smarter and more intentionally
  • Strategic thinking for your life, not just your business
  • Relationships, communication and emotional intelligence
  • Creating a personal plan for sustained mental fitness

Organisations

Business Coaching

A mentally fit team is not a luxury. It is the most sustainable competitive advantage a business can have. Mikal works with organisations at every level to build the mental fitness that drives genuine, durable performance.

Level One

Owner / CEO Coaching

The person at the top sets the tone for everything. Mikal works directly with owners and CEOs to develop their mental fitness, their leadership clarity and their capacity to navigate the unique pressures of being responsible for everything.

This is deep, personal work. The goal is not just better business performance - it is a more grounded, clear and fulfilled leader.

Level Two

Senior Management Coaching

Senior leaders carry enormous responsibility and often have the least support. Mikal works with leadership teams to build cohesion, communication and the mental resilience needed to perform consistently at the highest level.

Better leaders create better teams. The return on investment here ripples through the entire organisation.

Level Three

Whole Team Programmes

When the whole team understands mental fitness - what it is, how to build it and how to support each other in it - the culture shifts. Mikal designs bespoke programmes for whole-staff engagement that go beyond team-building exercises.

Real culture change requires everyone. Mikal has the experience to make that accessible and engaging at every level.

Immersive Experience

Private Retreats

Some things cannot happen in an hour. Some of the deepest work - the kind that genuinely changes a person's relationship with themselves and with life - requires time, space and sustained presence. That is what Mikal's private retreats offer.

Held across New Zealand's extraordinary natural landscapes, Mikal's retreats are deeply personalised experiences. He does not run a fixed curriculum. He creates a container - safe, thoughtful and unhurried - in which real transformation can occur.

Solo Retreats

Designed entirely around one person, a solo retreat with Mikal is one of the most intensive and rewarding experiences available. Over 7 to 10 days, Mikal works with you in a way that simply cannot happen across months of weekly sessions.

Group Retreats

Group retreats range from 1 to 10 days and can be designed for leadership teams, couples, small groups of friends or any gathering of people seeking something meaningful together. Mikal has deep experience in holding group spaces with skill and wisdom.
