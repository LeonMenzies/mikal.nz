Did You Know?

Time management doesn't work

In his book Time Management: A Complete Waste of Time, Mikal shares with us why traditional time management is fundamentally flawed — and what to replace it with.

The central insight: There will always be more things you want to do than there is time available to do them. Therefore, it is not a time issue but a priority issue.

In the pages of this book, you will discover exactly how to determine what is most important to you, as well as how to prioritise your daily actions to get the results you want.

Imagine starting your days relaxed, inspired, and with clarity about how to use this day of yours. Then imagine finishing your day with a smile on your face as you drift into a great night's sleep.

"As you turn these pages, you will debunk the myths of traditional time management, discover the power of purposeful living, and gain practical tools that you can implement immediately." — Ben Gioia, from the Foreword
