Personal & Life Coaching

Individual Coaching

My coaching is focussed on depth rather than width.

If you have that feeling that there has got to be more to life than what you are experiencing, you are absolutely right — and I can most likely help you in discovering more of your deepest potential.

The results from this may include:

  • Manage your everyday life with more ease and positivity
  • Discover what you are here in life to do and be
  • Learn simple ways to connect deeper with your spiritual essence
  • Realise the magic of being confident and relaxed at the same time
  • Realise that life really isn't that serious — and become more successful for the right reasons

Individual coaching is an ongoing affair, just like sports and any other skill-based coaching.

📍 Where & How

Individual coaching is done on Zoom. Some face-to-face sessions may be available in Auckland & Northland, New Zealand.

Depending on your situation, sessions are 1–2 hours each, mostly done weekly.

💰 Investment

I see coaching not as a cost but as an investment. I have personally invested more than $100,000 in my own personal growth journey.

Your investment will initially be between $1,000 and $2,000/month depending on the program and pace you choose.

Online Group Coaching

Your GPS for Life and Business

An 8-week online group coaching program combining 12 hours of group coaching with 2 hours of individual time with Mikal. Limited to 12 people.

Content & Outcomes

  • Finding or solidifying your purpose and vision
  • Discover your natural strengths and weaknesses
  • Understanding your own mindset and how it influences you
  • Tools for managing yourself, managing others, communication and conflict resolution
  • Tools for optimising your energy, mental, physical and emotional wellbeing
  • How to use mindfulness & meditation effectively
  • Understanding the ingredients of success

The GPS Framework

  • Clarity of Purpose — The WHY: Knowing whether to turn on the GPS
  • Know your True Starting Point: What the GPS does when you turn it on
  • Understand why you are where you are: Clarity on why you need the GPS
  • Where are you Going — Really: Select your destination
  • Map out How to get there: The GPS finds the best route
  • Accountability & Community: The GPS guides, points out wrong turns, finds alternatives

Business Coaching

If you want standard business coaching to improve your bottom line, please leave this website.

On the other hand, if you want support with running a business that is profoundly fulfilling for you and the staff — I know how to create and maintain that. A workplace that people want to be at, admired by the community, bringing the best out of everyone.

I offer business coaching at 3 core levels:

  • One-on-one with the owner/CEO/manager
  • Team sessions with the management/leadership team(s)
  • Team sessions with the staff

It is an organic process that evolves over time and is entirely tailored to your business and situation. It makes no difference what your business is doing, nor how small or big — my coaching is about the people.

Team Culture Development

Who benefits from a great business team culture? Everyone — you, your colleagues, customers, the bottom line, and your family.

In our adult working lives, there is nothing else that we spend more time doing in our awake hours than work. Therefore, being unhappy at work is a significant waste of life.

Happy people are:

More Productive
Better Problem Solvers
More Creative
Handle Pressure Better
Less Likely to Quit
Work Better Together

A Team Culture Development Program covers:

  • Creating a unique team language that is simple and encouraging
  • Creating staff engagement and alignment
  • Significantly increasing awareness of self and others
  • Understanding each team member's natural strengths and weaknesses
  • Understanding conflict — choosing worth-having conflict, learning how to handle it
  • Understanding and managing stress
  • Learning tools that benefit team members personally, both at work and at home

Programs run for an initial period of 3–12 months depending on circumstances.

Private Retreats

For individuals and groups who are serious about their personal and spiritual growth.

🏕️ Private Group Retreats

From 1 to 10 days, limited to a small number of people. Organised by any individual who wants to bring together a group to explore life in further depth.

Topics can include:

  • Managing stress and living with more peace and ease
  • Self-worth, confidence and assertiveness
  • Time management
  • Intimate relationships
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Physical, emotional and mental wellbeing
  • Spiritual awareness and growth

🧘 Private Solo Retreats

"My solo retreat with Mikal was an incredibly rich and worthwhile experience. Having one-on-one teaching for 10 days is very rare, and Mikal is very skilled at guiding effectively." — Eli Soothill, Solo Retreat Participant

Solo retreats are you and me together in a remote location for 2–10 days. No mobile phones, no TVs, no outside distractions.

This is total immersion into the essence of life — discovering and learning things that would otherwise take years in normal surroundings.

Investment

Solo retreats are typically 7–10 days. Depending on the length, venue and location, your investment is likely to be between NZ$15,000 and $20,000, including everything apart from your own travel costs.

How to Get There

Solo retreats are not available off-the-shelf. We start with one-on-one coaching (2–3 sessions per month) and build toward the retreat when the time is right — which may be months or years away.
