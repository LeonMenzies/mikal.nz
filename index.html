About Mikal

A revolution in thinking
and approach to life

My name is Mikal Nielsen, and for over 30 years I have followed my calling as a life coach — helping people get more life out of life, more business out of business, and more love out of relationships.

If you want a normal life, I'm definitely the wrong person for you. But if you are after extra-ordinary, I could be the one to help you discover your own key — and more importantly, how to use it to unlock what you are truly here in life to be and do.

No, it's not a big secret. You actually already know it and have the key yourself. Using it to unlock the true magic of life is, of course, another story.
