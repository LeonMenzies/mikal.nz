Who is Mikal?

Meet Mikal Nielsen

Danish-born. Earth citizen. 30+ years helping people discover what they are truly capable of.

The Man

"Dad's weird!" — and he's fine with that

Mikal is passionate about two things above everything else: his own growth, and helping others grow. He is relentlessly curious about how things work — people, systems, the quiet mechanics underneath a fulfilled life.

Born in Denmark, he became an earth citizen long before that was fashionable. He met his New Zealand wife while travelling and has built a life in Northland shaped by perspectives from over 50 countries.

He is a husband, a dad, a cafe-goer, a kitchen experimenter, a barefoot runner, and a skier who still gets genuinely excited about fresh snow.

Work with Mikal
The Turning Point

From shy and bullied — to confident and free

Mikal was not always the confident presence he is today. As a child, he was shy and bullied. His journey to the person he is now began with a profound personal experience at the age of 27 — one that fundamentally changed his understanding of what human beings are capable of.

That experience informs everything he does. His coaching is not theoretical — it is rooted in a lived understanding of what it takes to genuinely transform.

He has appeared on national television, radio, and in newspapers in New Zealand. He has written 4 books — 2 published independently and 2 co-authored with his wife.

"A revolution in thinking and approach to life, business and intimacy is desperately needed. Most people are living a fraction of what is possible — and they don't even know it."
Mikal Nielsen
What Drives Him

Thirty years of coaching. Still curious.

Mikal's 30-year coaching career has taken him across industries and life stages — business owners, CEOs, parents, couples, people who have everything on paper and feel nothing inside.

He has not stopped learning. He meditates. He runs barefoot. He skis. He cooks. He reads. He keeps growing — because he believes that the coach who stops growing stops being useful.

Based in Ruakaka, Northland. Available worldwide via Zoom.

Life Outside Coaching

The adventurer who practises what he preaches

Mikal doesn't just talk about balance and wellbeing — he lives it. You'll find him on the slopes in winter, running barefoot on Northland trails, or exploring a new country.

This isn't performance. It is integration — the same integration he helps clients create in their own lives.
