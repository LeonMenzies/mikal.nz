INsight, INspiration, INtentional Action

Conversations for leaders, entrepreneurs, and anyone serious about living a mentally fit life.

About the Show

Real conversations. Real transformation.

The Mental Fitness Podcast is Mikal Nielsen's platform for exploring the interior life of high-performing individuals — business owners, entrepreneurs, athletes, and people navigating life's hardest moments.

Each episode goes beneath the surface: past the productivity tactics and into the thinking, the beliefs, and the inner work that separates people who are genuinely fulfilled from those who are merely busy.

36 episodes. Two seasons. Guests from around the world.
