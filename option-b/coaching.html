One-on-One

Personal Coaching for Individuals

Depth over width. Mikal's personal coaching goes beneath the surface to where real change lives. Not quick fixes or performance hacks — genuine transformation in how you think, feel, and operate.

Results include: managing life with more ease, discovering real purpose, connecting spiritually, and becoming confident AND relaxed — not as opposites but as the same thing.

Coaching is ongoing — like a sports coach, not a one-session wonder. Sessions run 1–2 hours, mostly weekly.

Practical Details

  • Via Zoom or face-to-face in Auckland/Northland
  • Sessions: 1–2 hours, mostly weekly
  • Investment: NZ$1,000–$2,000/month

Group Coaching

Your GPS for Life and Business

8 weeks. 12 hours group coaching + 2 hours individual. Limited to 12 people.

The GPS Framework

  • 01 Clarity of Purpose — your WHY
  • 02 Your True Starting Point
  • 03 Why you are where you are
  • 04 Where you are going
  • 05 How to get there
  • 06 Accountability & Community

Topics: purpose, vision, strengths, weaknesses, mindset, managing self and others, communication, conflict, stress, energy, mindfulness, meditation, physical wellbeing, ingredients of success.

Programme Details

  • 8 weeks total duration
  • 12 hrs group + 2 hrs individual coaching
  • Maximum 12 people (min 4)
  • Weekly 90-min Zoom: Wed 8–9:30am NZ
  • Personal journal + private community
  • US$1,195/person (NZ$1,495+GST)
  • Bring a friend: 20% off. Bring 5: free spot

Business Coaching

Coaching for Businesses

Note: This is not standard business coaching focused purely on the bottom line. If that is what you are looking for, this is not the right fit. Mikal's business coaching is about people — and people are always the real business.

Three Levels of Engagement

  • One-on-one with the owner or CEO
  • Team sessions with senior management
  • Team sessions with all staff

Organic and tailored. It is always about the people.

Team Culture Development

3–12 month programme. Areas covered:

  • Shared team language
  • Engagement and alignment
  • Self-awareness and emotional intelligence
  • Strengths and weaknesses
  • Conflict and communication
  • Stress management and resilience
"This has been the most impactful and successful coaching experience that I have had." Bruce Larsen, Managing Director, Northpine

Why Mental Fitness in Business Matters

Mentally fit people are more productive, better problem solvers, more creative, handle pressure better, take less sick leave, and are less likely to quit. Happy people are good business — and building happy, mentally fit teams is exactly what Mikal does.

Immersive Growth

Private Retreats

For those who are serious about personal and spiritual growth. These are not off-the-shelf packages.

Group Retreats

Small groups. 1–10 days. Topics may include:

  • Stress and inner peace
  • Self-worth and confidence
  • Time management and priorities
  • Relationships and intimacy
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Physical, emotional and spiritual wellbeing

Solo Retreats

You and Mikal. A remote location. 2–10 days. No phones, no TV, no distraction.

Solo retreats require an established coaching relationship. Start with 2–3 sessions per month of individual coaching.

Investment

NZ$15,000–$20,000 for 7–10 days (excluding travel).
