New Release

Time Management: A Complete Waste of Time

Reduce Stress. Gain Control. Find Inner Peace.

About the Book

The book that challenges everything you think you know about time

Time management doesn't work. It never has. You have read the books, downloaded the apps, colour-coded the calendar — and still you feel behind, overwhelmed, and vaguely dissatisfied with how your days are going.

Mikal Nielsen spent 30 years watching smart, driven people torture themselves with productivity systems that were solving the wrong problem. This book is the result of that observation.

What if the issue was never time? What if it was clarity — about what matters, who you are, and what kind of life you actually want to live?

With a foreword by Ben Gioia, this book introduces the Life Mastery Framework: a completely different approach that starts with purpose and ends with genuine inner peace.

Inside the Book

What You Will Discover

This is not a time management book. It is a thinking book — one that dismantles the myths and replaces them with something that actually works.

The 7 Myths of Time Management

Mikal identifies the seven deeply embedded beliefs about time that are keeping you stuck — and shows exactly why each one is a lie.

The Life Mastery Framework

A complete alternative system built around purpose, priorities, and presence. Not a to-do list — a way of being that transforms how you operate every day.

Why Stress Is a Choice

Discover the real source of stress and why conventional approaches to managing it make it worse. Learn the mental fitness tools that change everything.

Gaining Real Control

Control is not about doing more faster. It is about knowing what matters and having the mental clarity to act on it. This section shows you how.

Finding Inner Peace

The unexpected destination of this journey: a settled, purposeful, genuinely peaceful relationship with your time and your life.

Practical Tools That Stick

Every insight comes with practical application. These are not exercises you do once — they are habits that compound over months and years.

"Stop managing time. Start mastering life. The difference is not a productivity hack — it is a revolution in thinking."
Mikal Nielsen, Author
Who It's For

Written for people who are tired of the same old answers

This book is for business owners, leaders, parents, and professionals who feel like they are running hard but getting nowhere. People who have tried the planners and the apps and the early morning routines — and still feel behind.

It is also for anyone who senses that there is something more — a different way of moving through life that combines high performance with genuine peace. That is not a fantasy. It is a skill set, and it is learnable.

If you are ready to stop tinkering with your system and start changing your thinking, this book is for you.
