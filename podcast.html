About the Podcast

Monologues and Dialogues that matter

The Mental Fitness Podcast covers topics like leadership, productivity, time management, work-life balance and challenges like stress, conflict and mental health — and much more.

Episodes are a mix of solo monologues where Mikal shares his thinking and frameworks, and conversations with guests — business owners, coaches and individuals who have gone through real transformation.

No fluff. No filler. Just the kind of insight that sticks.

Appear on the Show

Become a Podcast Guest

The Mental Fitness Podcast features conversations with people who have powerful stories and insights to share. Guest conversations follow three key threads:

  1. Your story — the challenge, the turning point, the transformation
  2. What you learned — the tools, mindset shifts and practices that made the difference
  3. Your message — the one thing you'd want listeners to take away

If you have a genuine transformation story or powerful insight to share with business owners and leaders, get in touch.
