About Mikal

The Mind Mechanic

"Helping high performers get more life out of life"

Born in Denmark, Mikal went from a super shy, withdrawn and bullied child to confident public speaker and coach — following a transformative experience at age 27. He describes himself as "an earth citizen, not belonging to any country, culture, religion or political orientation." He has visited over 50 countries and now lives in Ruakaka, Northland, New Zealand.

His coaching is focused on depth rather than width. Over 30 years he has helped business owners and leaders discover what they are here in life to do and be — and build businesses that are profoundly fulfilling for themselves and their teams.

Featured on NZ National TV, Radio, newspapers and magazines. Author of four books (two published). International keynote and public speaker. Creator of the Mental Fitness podcast, now in Season 2.
