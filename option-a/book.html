New Book

Time Management: A Complete Waste of Time

Reduce Stress · Gain Control · Find Inner Peace

The Book

Did you know? Time management doesn't work.

There will always be more things you want to do than time available. This is not a time problem — it is a priority problem. And yet we keep buying planners, apps, and systems that promise to solve a problem that cannot be solved with more organisation.

In this book, Mikal debunks the myths of traditional time management and introduces something more powerful: a framework for purposeful living that leaves you feeling genuinely in control — not because you squeezed more into the day, but because you stopped trying to.

With a foreword by Ben Gioia, the book takes you through the Life Mastery Framework — a proven approach to clarity, priorities, and the inner peace that comes from living in alignment with what actually matters to you.

What You Will Discover

Debunk the Myths

You will uncover the seven myths of traditional time management that are actively working against you — and why they feel convincing even as they fail.

The Power of Purposeful Living

Discover how clarity of purpose transforms everything — not just what you do with your time, but how you feel about your life as a whole.

Practical Tools

Gain practical, immediately applicable tools that help you focus on what matters, let go of what doesn't, and experience genuine peace — not just productivity.
