Public Speaking

The Un-Motivational Speaker

No hype. No ra-ra. Thought-provoking, edge-of-seat presentations that awaken potential and create real dialogue.

A Different Kind of Speaker

Not an entertainer. Not a motivator. Something better.

Mikal is not an entertainer, and he is not a motivational speaker. He will not leave your audience pumped up for 48 hours before the feeling wears off and nothing has changed.

He is thought-provoking. He is edge-of-seat. He awakens potential and creates genuine dialogue. He gives your audience information and perspective they will be thinking about long after the event is over.

He has been described as an "un-motivational speaker" — and he takes that as a compliment. No hype, no ra-ra: just fantastic, applicable information delivered in a style that is uniquely his own.

Mikal is listed on the Speakers New Zealand platform and has presented to business groups, industry associations, chambers of commerce, and corporate audiences across New Zealand.

The Experience

Audiences won't forget it

Mikal has a gift for using the unexpected to unlock the profound. His presentations include moments — like the famous arrow story — that audience members describe years later as turning points in how they think.

Fiona Jones recalled: "The arrow-breaking story wasn't just impressive — it completely changed how I understand the role of belief in performance and life. I think about it regularly."
