One-on-One

Personal Coaching for Individuals

Mikal's personal coaching is focused on depth rather than width. It is not about checking boxes or learning frameworks — it is about uncovering what is genuinely holding you back, and what would genuinely fulfil you.

The results clients experience include: managing life with more ease, discovering a sense of purpose, connecting more deeply with themselves and others, and becoming confident — and relaxed at the same time. Not either/or. Both.

Coaching is ongoing, like working with a sports coach. You wouldn't train once and expect peak performance — the same is true here.

Practical Details

  • Conducted via Zoom, with some face-to-face options in Auckland and Northland
  • Sessions are 1–2 hours, mostly weekly
  • Investment: NZ$1,000–$2,000/month depending on programme

Group Coaching

Your GPS for Life and Business

An 8-week intensive group coaching programme, limited to 12 people. Real transformation, in community.

What You Will Cover

The programme takes you through the GPS Framework — a structured journey from where you are to where you truly want to be:

  • 1. Clarity of Purpose — your WHY
  • 2. Your True Starting Point — honest self-assessment
  • 3. Why you are where you are — removing blind spots
  • 4. Where you are going — a vision that genuinely excites you
  • 5. How to get there — practical tools and strategies
  • 6. Accountability and Community — staying the course

Topics include: purpose and vision, natural strengths and weaknesses, mindset tools, managing self and others, communication, conflict resolution, energy, mental and physical and emotional wellbeing, mindfulness and meditation, and the ingredients of success.

Programme Details

  • 8 weeks total
  • 12 hours group coaching + 2 hours individual
  • Limited to 12 people (minimum 4)
  • Weekly 90-min Zoom, Wednesdays 8–9:30am NZ time

Business Coaching

Coaching for Businesses

Important note: This is not standard business coaching focused on the bottom line. If that is what you are looking for, this is not the right fit. If you are interested in building a profoundly fulfilling business — for yourself and your people — read on.

Three Levels of Engagement

  • One-on-one with the owner or CEO
  • Team sessions with senior management
  • Team sessions with all staff

The process is organic and tailored to each business. It is about the people — because it is always about the people.

Team Culture Development

A structured team programme running 3–12 months. Covers:

  • Shared team language and values
  • Engagement and alignment
  • Self-awareness and emotional intelligence
  • Natural strengths and weaknesses
  • Conflict management and communication
  • Stress management and resilience
"This has been the most impactful and successful coaching experience that I have had. Mikal's ability to identify and work through the real issues is extraordinary." Bruce Larsen, Managing Director, Northpine

Why Happy People Are Good Business

Research consistently shows that people who feel fulfilled are more productive, better at solving problems, more creative under pressure, better at handling stress, less likely to get sick, and less likely to quit. The wellbeing of your team is not a soft issue — it is a business issue.

Immersive Experience

Private Retreats

Private retreats are for those who are serious about their personal and spiritual growth. They are not off-the-shelf packages.

Group Retreats

Small groups, 1–10 days. Topics vary and may include:

  • Stress and inner peace
  • Self-worth and confidence
  • Time management and priorities
  • Relationships and intimacy
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Physical, emotional, mental and spiritual wellbeing

Solo Retreats

Just you and Mikal. A remote location. 2–10 days. No phones, no television, no distraction — just deep, focused work.

Solo retreats are not offered to new clients. They require an established coaching relationship — typically 2–3 sessions per month over a period of time. Mikal needs to know you, and you need to know him.

Investment

NZ$15,000–$20,000 for a 7–10 day retreat, excluding travel costs.

"My solo retreat with Mikal was an incredibly rich and worthwhile experience. He is very skilled at guiding effectively without imposing his own agenda." Eli Soothill

The best way to begin is with one-on-one coaching. If a retreat is right for you, the path will become clear from there.
