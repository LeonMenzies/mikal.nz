Public Speaking & Keynotes

The "Un-Motivational" Speaker

No hype. No ra-ra. Just thought-provoking content that gets people on the edge of their seats and makes a lasting difference.

The Approach

Why Mikal is different

If you are looking for an entertainer or motivational speaker for your business event, I'm not that one.

Why not? Two reasons:

  1. The world is full of those public and keynote speakers — no more are needed
  2. It rarely has a sustainable and lasting positive impact — which is the opposite of what my life and business is about

On the other hand, if you are looking for something that is:

  • Thought-provoking and makes the audience get on the edge of their seats
  • Awakens people's potential
  • Creates dialogue and discussion afterwards
  • The audience won't forget

…then I'm that speaker.

And, it will be entertaining — but not for the usual reasons.
